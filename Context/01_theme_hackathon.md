# Thème du hackathon — référence produit

## Theme
Tech for All — AI That Makes Life Easier for Older Adults

## Core challenge
Create AI tools that simplify digital tasks, enhance safety, and support independence for elders.

Many older adults face barriers to using technology effectively, which makes everyday digital tasks stressful or inaccessible.

## Common problems highlighted by the brief
- difficulty navigating smartphones, apps, and online services
- forgetting passwords, appointments, or important reminders
- feeling unsafe due to scams, fraud, or misinformation
- trouble managing health routines or daily life tasks
- interfaces that are not senior-friendly: small text, complex menus, poor accessibility

## Mission for teams
Build an AI-powered solution that helps older adults stay safe, independent, and confident with technology.

The product should:
- simplify life
- reduce digital stress
- make technology feel friendly and accessible

## Product interpretation adopted in this project
In this project, the theme is interpreted as follows:
- the product must be useful every day, not only during emergencies
- accessibility and low cognitive load are first-class requirements
- AI must help simplify, prioritize, summarize, or guide
- the solution must remain understandable and usable by seniors with low digital literacy
- the family / guardian loop is part of the value proposition
